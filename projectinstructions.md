## 1. Project Overview

The Web-Based Equipment Management System is a centralized platform designed to streamline the tracking, allocation, and maintenance of equipment within an organization. The system will provide real-time updates on equipment availability, support automated notifications for maintenance schedules, and offer reporting tools for data-driven decision-making. By moving away from manual tracking, this system aims to reduce operational inefficiencies, minimize losses, and enhance accountability.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Develop a secure, user-friendly, web-based system accessible from any device with an internet connection.
- Automate equipment tracking, allocation, and maintenance scheduling.
- Generate detailed reports on equipment usage, maintenance history, and inventory status.
- Improve accountability through audit trails and user activity logs.
- Reduce operational downtime by providing timely alerts for equipment maintenance.

---

## 3. System Features

- **User Authentication & Role Management** – Secure login with roles such as Admin, Staff, and Maintenance Personnel.
- **Equipment Inventory Management** – Add, edit, delete, and categorize equipment items.
- **Allocation & Reservation System** – Reserve or assign equipment to users with approval workflows.
- **Maintenance Tracking** – Schedule regular maintenance and track repair history.
- **Notifications & Alerts** – Email/SMS notifications for overdue returns, maintenance due dates, and equipment requests.
- **Reporting & Analytics** – Generate reports on utilization, maintenance, and inventory status.
- **Search & Filter Functionality** – Quick search for equipment by type, status, or location.

---

## 4. Functional Workflow

1. **User Login** – Users log in to the system according to their role.
2. **Equipment Management** – Admins update equipment inventory, add new items, or remove obsolete ones.
3. **Equipment Request & Allocation** – Staff requests equipment; Admin approves allocation based on availability.
4. **Maintenance Scheduling** – Maintenance personnel schedule regular servicing and log repairs.
5. **Notification System** – Automated alerts sent for pending returns, maintenance, or requests.
6. **Reporting** – Admins generate reports to analyze equipment usage and maintenance performance.

---

## 5. Expected Outcomes

- Efficient tracking of all organizational equipment in real-time.
- Reduced equipment loss and misuse through accountability measures.
- Improved maintenance management and reduced equipment downtime.
- Enhanced decision-making through data-driven insights from reports.
- Streamlined equipment request and approval process, saving time and effort.
